Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you register: name, email address, age, height, weight, fitness goals, and training preferences. We also collect workout session data you log within the app.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the Trackstar service — syncing your workouts, generating personalised plans, and enabling coach-athlete features. We do not sell your personal data.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat Manages in-app subscriptions. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Serves ads to free-tier users. May use an advertising identifier (IDFA) with your consent. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In / Sign in with Apple Optional authentication. Only your name and email are retrieved.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers hosted on Railway (cloud infrastructure). Workout data is also cached locally on your device.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Trackstar is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us.
